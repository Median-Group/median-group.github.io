Donate

The simplest way to donate is PayPal (donate@mediangroup.org). There is a 2.9% donation fee. Alternatively, contact info@mediangroup.org about alternative donation methods, such as cryptocurrency and wire transfer (which has a lower effective fee than PayPal for donations over $1000). Note that donating appreciated assets such as cryptocurrency directly can reduce capital gains tax, at least in the US.
